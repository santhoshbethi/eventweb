Frequently asked questions

1. What are Backpacking events?

Events that occur at a distance more than 500 kms (subjected to changes) from any given location and usually require more than a day to conclude are known as backpacking events. Backpacking events include events like trekking, hiking, etc.

 2. What are City Freak events?

Events that occur within the city limits or the events usually concluding within 24 hours from the time of commencement are known as city freak events. City freak events include events like concerts, exhibitions, etc.

 3. What are Sports Events?

Events involving activities like physical exertion or skill or both are known as a sports events.Sports events include sports and games like cricket, volleyball, chess etc.

 4. What are Social / Education events?

Events involving activities that directly or indirectly help for transforming the society or something that is conducted on an educational purpose are known as social / Education events.Social/ Education events include events such as promotion of education, promoting gender equality etc.

18. What is a transaction ID ?

The transaction ID is a special set of numbers that defines each transaction. With its help, bank workers can identify the purchase that was made by the customer. The transaction ID is always unique, which means that there are no transaction IDs that are the same.
